Transport Hub quick guide

Use this page as a reference while onboarding new admins, teachers, or drivers.

  1. Set windows first: Open Operations and confirm tracking windows + days.
  2. Register fleet: In Vehicle Registry add each bus, owner, and insurance.
  3. Create routes & stops: Define Routes, then map Stops with GPS.
  4. Allocate students: Use Allocations to link each learner to a route and vehicle.
  5. Teach the driver: Send driver to transportdriver/transporthub.html and ensure GPS permissions are granted.
  6. Monitor boarding: Gate tablets use Boarding Gate to enforce “No Pay, No Board”.
  7. Handle requests: Approve Fuel, Maintenance, and Payment queues daily.
  8. Review incidents: Log issues immediately inside Incidents to maintain a safety trail.
  9. Keep parents informed: Configure push templates in Notifications and verify FCM tokens on parent devices.
